About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the College of Intelligence and Computing at Tianjin University, where I work with the EdgeBigBang Group under the supervision of Professor Xiaofei Wang.

My research explores the intersection of machine learning and systems, with a focus on building intelligent, high-performance cloud and edge infrastructures that deliver high throughput and SLO compliance in dynamic multi-tenant environments. My current work centers on workload forecasting and unified frameworks for LLM inference across distributed platforms.
